Who We Are

Built for Sierra Leone's Students

EduSL is a free digital education platform designed to bridge the learning gap across Sierra Leone. Many students in Freetown and beyond struggle to access quality study materials we're changing that.

Whether you're preparing for BECE, WASSCE, or a university exam, EduSL brings notes, past questions, video lessons, and assignments to your fingertips.

  • ✅ 100% Free for all students
  • ✅ Works on mobile phones
  • ✅ Covers JSS, SSS, and University
  • ✅ Updated by Sierra Leonean teachers
